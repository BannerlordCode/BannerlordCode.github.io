## 对 modder 的影响 / Impact for modders

**中文：** ⚠️ 1.4.5 变化最大：新增 `EffectiveFirstMate`/`EffectiveNavigator` 及 `SetPartyFirstMate`/`SetPartyNavigator`、`RemoveAllPartyRolesOfHero`、`GetHeroPartyRoles` 等一批成员，构成新的“船队角色”系统；同时移除 `GetHeroPartyRole`/`RemoveHeroPartyRole`（重构为新 API）。涉及队伍角色分配的 mod 必须迁移到 FirstMate/Navigator 接口。

**English:** ⚠️ Largest change in 1.4.5: adds `EffectiveFirstMate`/`EffectiveNavigator` plus `SetPartyFirstMate`/`SetPartyNavigator`, `RemoveAllPartyRolesOfHero`, `GetHeroPartyRoles` and more — a new party-role system; removes `GetHeroPartyRole`/`RemoveHeroPartyRole` (refactored into the new API). Mods assigning party roles must migrate to the FirstMate/Navigator interface.
